About HSC

This monsoon, we at the historic security council of DPSMUN aim, not only to recreate challenging geopolitical and intellectually stimulating crises to which plausible and effective solutions must be found, but also to view the world through the eyes of a leader. A delegate must know how to tackle sensitive issues diplomatically and choose any course of action wisely.

The Historic Security Council will be simulated in October 1956, a time ripe with global tensions and unforeseeable possibilities. Revolutions on one side of the world, and incursions on another. The Suez Crisis, as it is commonly referred to as was a situation precedented by the first signs of the apparent decline of the role of the United Kingdom in the global geopolitics, and the Hungarian Revolution served as the first signs of the collapse of the Soviet Union.

The purpose of simulating a historic conference on matters of such monumental importance is not merely, as the cliché goes, to learn from our mistakes. History is infact, an exemplar problem for us to scrutinize and change. To contemplate all alternate outcomes. The purpose of this Historic Security Council is to provide delegates with a platform where they are expected to do exactly that. To contemplate all possible elements and then arrive at the most feasible outcome, which of course pertains to their nation’s interests. The HSC at DPSMUN promises to be perhaps the most challenging conference a delegate may have faced. Because within the span of two months, the world saw itself faced with not just one, but two crises of paramount importance in the present day geopolitics. Thus the onus was upon the United Nations Security Council to ensure that the threat to global peace and security is eliminated peacefully and diplomatically.

So delegates, come prepared. Because, you will undoubtedly be tested. Where your actions are at par with your words, and your decisions affect the global system as a whole.

We look forward to a highly competitive committee and of course being surprised with your solutions to this complicated issue.
